Indian Air Force

About

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces and plays a crucial role in safeguarding the nation's airspace. Established on October 8, 1932, the IAF has grown into one of the most powerful and technologically advanced air forces in the world. Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and conduct aerial warfare during armed conflicts.
