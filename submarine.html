Submarine Overview

INS Kalvari

INS Kalvari is a Scorpene-class submarine built in India under Project 75. It's equipped with advanced stealth features and is capable of powerful underwater and surface attacks using torpedoes and missiles.

It enhances India's naval deterrence and underwater warfare capabilities, contributing to maritime security in the Indian Ocean region.
